**Quick Take 3 (Thursday):**

If I could only teach one PM skill:

It wouldn't be frameworks. It wouldn't be SQL. It wouldn't be "stakeholder management."

It would be this: learn to sit in disagreement without rushing to resolve it.

The best PMs hold multiple contradicting ideas simultaneously. Then pick one and ship.

**Quick Take 5 (Next Monday):**

Something I noticed building PM Multiverse:

When you force people to answer BEFORE showing expert opinions, they think harder.

When you show experts first, they just anchor on whoever they admire most.

Design insight: commitment before revelation > revelation before commitment.
