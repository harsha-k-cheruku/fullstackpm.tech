---

**Tweet 2:**

The problem with PM case studies: they give you a scenario then tell you the "right" answer.

Real PM work isn't like that. Smart people disagree. Every day.

So I built a tool that simulates the disagreement.

---

**Tweet 3:**

5 personas, each with a real PM philosophy:

📚 Cagan — validate before you ship
🔭 Torres — talk to users every week
⚔️ Doshi — find the sharpest wedge
📈 Lenny — build the growth loop
⚡ The Exec — ship in 2 weeks, learn live

They look at the same problem. They disagree.

---

**Tweet 4:**

The UX flow:

1. Pick a real PM problem (YouTube Brazil, Netflix Podcasts, etc.)
2. Answer 3 questions BEFORE seeing expert takes
3. See your "PM DNA" — which persona you think like
4. Watch 5 experts argue
5. Vote on whose approach you'd run
6. Unlock your shareable DNA card

---

**Tweet 5:**

Biggest surprise: Doshi wins a lot of votes.

PMs apparently love the "find the sharpest wedge and go all in" approach more than I expected.

The "validate everything first" camp is smaller than I thought.

---

**Tweet 6:**

Tech stack: zero frameworks.

- Vanilla JS (no React/Vue)
- JSON files (no database for content)
- localStorage (no user accounts)
- One HTML file per page
- Built in 14 hours

Sometimes the simplest tech is the right tech.
