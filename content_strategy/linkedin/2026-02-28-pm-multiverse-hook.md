Most PM case studies have the same problem:

They give you a scenario. Then they tell you the "right" answer.

That's not how product management works.

In real life, smart people disagree. The VP wants to ship fast. The research lead wants another round of discovery. The growth PM wants to run an experiment. The founder wants to bet on a wedge.

Nobody's wrong. They're just optimizing for different things.

So I built a tool that captures that tension.

PM Multiverse puts you inside real PM problems — YouTube launching in Brazil, Netflix exploring podcasts, Slack fixing onboarding — and lets 5 distinct PM voices argue about what to do.

You answer first. Then you watch them disagree. Then you vote.

No right answers. Just trade-offs.
