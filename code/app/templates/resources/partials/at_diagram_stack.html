Programmatic Stack — Layer Map

The adtech stack is a relay: advertiser demand enters on one side, publisher inventory exits on the other, with auctions and verification in between.

Advertiser Side

Brands and agencies define budget, audience, and campaign goals (awareness, traffic, conversion).

BrandAgencyTrading Desk

Demand Side (DSP)

DSPs evaluate bid opportunities and buy inventory programmatically with real-time algorithms.

Google DV360The Trade DeskAmazon DSP

Exchange Layer

Ad exchanges run auctions across connected DSP demand and SSP supply. Data layers enrich targeting.

Google AdXOpenXIndex ExchangeDMP/CDP/Clean Rooms

Supply Side (SSP)

SSPs package publisher inventory, connect it to exchanges, and optimize yield/fill.

Google Ad ManagerMagnitePubMatic

Publisher Side

Sites/apps expose ad slots and monetize user attention via programmatic demand.

PublisherWebsite/AppAd SlotUser

Verification & Measurement

Third parties validate viewability, brand safety, invalid traffic, and campaign reach quality.

IASDoubleVerifyMOATNielsen / Comscore

PM lens: every layer claims optimization. Your product strategy depends on which side of the value chain you own and where margin leaks occur.
