Optional: Softened Black vs Pure Black

Your call: Pure black (#000000) on pure white (#FFFFFF) gives maximum contrast (21:1 ratio). Some find this slightly harsh for long-form reading. Below is a comparison — pure black vs. a slightly softened #111111 for body text. Both are WCAG AAA compliant.

Pure Black Text (#000000)

Product Managers who want to become Full Stack PMs lack a clear roadmap. PMs don't know which technical skills matter most. SDEs don't know which product skills to develop first. Both groups need practical, experience-based guidance — not generic career advice.

Softened Black Text (#111111)

Product Managers who want to become Full Stack PMs lack a clear roadmap. PMs don't know which technical skills matter most. SDEs don't know which product skills to develop first. Both groups need practical, experience-based guidance — not generic career advice.
