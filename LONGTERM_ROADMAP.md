---

## The Model (How This Actually Makes Money)

Most creator/coaching businesses follow this revenue stack — you build bottom-up:

```
LEVEL 4: Community ($50-100/mo subscription)     ← Month 24-36
LEVEL 3: Cohort / Group Program ($500-2K)        ← Month 12-24
LEVEL 2: Course / Digital Product ($97-497)      ← Month 12-18
LEVEL 1: 1:1 Coaching ($200-500/hr)              ← Month 6-12
LEVEL 0: Book + Free Tools (existing)            ← NOW
```

You don't skip levels. Each level funds the next and builds the audience for it.
The book and free tools (PM Multiverse, Interview Coach) are already at Level 0.
The roadmap is about climbing the stack deliberately.

---

## The Audience (Most Important Decision)

Everything else flows from this. Based on your background:

**Primary audience:**
> PMs and aspiring PMs who want technical depth — to build, ship, and close
> the gap between "I have an idea" and "it's live."

**Specific personas:**
- **Aspiring PM** — engineer or analyst who wants to break into product
- **Junior PM (0-3 yrs)** — learning fundamentals, preparing for interviews
- **Mid-level PM (3-7 yrs)** — wants to add technical/AI skills to stay relevant
- **Senior PM** — wants to build their own thing on the side

**You are NOT trying to reach:**
- Recruiters (that was Option A)
- Other coaches (vanity audience)
- General "tech" audience (too broad)

**Your unfair advantage in this space:**
SDE → PM → Builder is a rare arc. Most PM educators are either ex-engineers who
never did PM, or ex-PMs who can't code. You're both. That's your moat.

## Phase 3: First Monetization
### Months 9-18 | "Sell something small first"

**The goal:** First $2K/month in revenue. Validate that people will pay for
your expertise before building a large course.

### 3.1 Start with 1:1 Coaching (Month 9-12)
This is the fastest path to revenue with a small audience.

**Offer:**
- PM career coaching / interview prep
- $200-300/hour
- "Full Stack PM" coaching — helping PMs add technical depth
- Book via Calendly, pay upfront

**How to launch:**
- One LinkedIn post: "I'm opening 4 coaching spots for PM interview prep.
  $250/hour. Here's what we'll cover: [list]. DM me."
- Add coaching page to site with Calendly + Stripe
- With 500 email subscribers, you need 3% to convert = 15 inquiries → 6-8 clients

**Revenue target:** 4-6 clients/month × $300 = $1.2K-1.8K/month

### 3.2 First Cohort / Workshop (Month 12-15)
Once you have 5-10 coaching clients, you know what they're asking for.
Package that into a small group experience.

**Offer idea:** "Full Stack PM Bootcamp — 4 weeks"
- Week 1: Product thinking + frameworks
- Week 2: Technical depth for PMs (APIs, databases, AI tools)
- Week 3: Build a simple product with AI (like PM Multiverse)
- Week 4: Ship, measure, iterate

**Pricing:** $500-800 per person
**Cohort size:** 10-15 people
**Revenue:** 12 people × $600 = $7.2K per cohort

**How to fill it:**
- Email list (500+ subs at this point)
- LinkedIn announcement
- PM Multiverse users (built-in warm audience)

### 3.3 Digital Product / Course (Month 12-18)
A self-paced course is lower effort to run than a cohort but needs
more audience to sell without live launch energy.

**Course idea:** "The Full Stack PM Toolkit"
- How to build PM intuition
- SQL + Python for PMs (without becoming an engineer)
- Using AI tools to ship 10x faster
- Case studies: how I built PM Multiverse, Interview Coach, etc.

**Pricing:** $197-397
**Platform:** Gumroad (simple) or Teachable/Maven (more infrastructure)

**Don't build this until:**
- You have 500+ email subscribers
- You've done 5+ coaching sessions (so you know what to teach)
- You've validated the topic via LinkedIn posts that got traction

### 3.4 Book as Part of the Funnel
The book is already done — make it work harder:
- [ ] Mention it in every newsletter
- [ ] Add to email signature
- [ ] LinkedIn posts about specific lessons from the book
- [ ] Reach out to PM podcasts to be a guest (book = credibility hook)

**Phase 3 Success Metrics:**
- 1K+ email subscribers
- $2K/month in revenue (coaching + course + book)
- First cohort completed
- 2-3 podcast guest appearances
- 5K monthly site visitors

## Phase 4: Scale
### Months 18-36 | "Build the recurring revenue layer"

**The goal:** $10K+/month with recurring revenue that doesn't require
trading all your time for money.

### 4.1 Productize Coaching (Month 18-24)

Move from hourly → packaged:
- **3-month Full Stack PM accelerator** — $1,500-2,000 total
- Bi-weekly calls + async feedback + access to course materials
- Target: 8-10 active clients at any time
- Revenue: 8 × $1,800 / 3 months = ~$4.8K/month

### 4.2 Community (Month 24-36)

A paid community is recurring revenue that doesn't require you to
show up every week like coaching does.

**Model:** Paid Slack or Circle community
**Pricing:** $49-99/month
**What members get:**
- Monthly live Q&A with you
- Peer community of Full Stack PMs
- Early access to courses/tools
- Monthly reading stack + newsletter

**Revenue:** 100 members × $69/month = $6.9K/month recurring
**How to get there:** Email list of 2K+ + brand + 12 months of trust-building

### 4.3 Sponsorships (Month 24+)

Once you have 2K+ email subscribers and consistent traffic:
- Newsletter sponsorships ($200-500 per issue)
- Tool/platform sponsorships (relevant PM tools: Figma, Linear, Notion, etc.)

Don't pursue this early — it's a distraction with a small audience.

### 4.4 Site Roadmap for Scale

Features to build as the business grows (don't build these now):
- [ ] Email capture + Beehiiv integration (Phase 1)
- [ ] Coaching booking page with Calendly + Stripe (Phase 3)
- [ ] Course hosting (Phase 3) — probably external (Gumroad/Teachable)
- [ ] Community page + link (Phase 4)
- [ ] Member login / gated content (Phase 4)

**Phase 4 Success Metrics:**
- $10K+/month revenue
- 2K+ email subscribers
- 100+ community members
- Course running on autopilot
- Coaching by referral only (demand > supply)

## The One Thing That Kills This Plan

**Inconsistency.**

The biggest risk isn't competition, isn't the wrong niche, isn't the wrong
product. It's writing 6 posts and stopping. Posting for 3 weeks and stopping.
Starting a newsletter and sending 2 issues.

Every creator who builds to $10K/month did one thing: they kept going when
it felt like nothing was working. The compounding only shows up after 6-12
months of consistent output.

The week-level plan we build next should be ruthlessly focused on
protecting the content output above everything else.
